Don't Call it a Comeback

Thu 24 January 2013
By tBunnyMan

In News.

We were never gone!

I know you have all been waiting too long for the results of the contest and we're really sorry that hasn't been announced. Sadly this isn't the announcement of the winner but it is still coming soon. This is just a quick update since we have left you all hanging.

Internally we had suffered some setbacks as some people have dropped from the project before it really got off the ground. However we have been working to fill those gaps and put ourselves not just right back on track but far ahead! We have some new tentative commitments to the convention and lots of offers for help recently. If you still want to help put on local events or volunteer for the convention please email staff at vegasfur dot com with any information and we will make sure to get back to you quickly!

We are going to try and commit to posting on a regular basis with at least some news just so we don't leave everyone hanging for so long.
