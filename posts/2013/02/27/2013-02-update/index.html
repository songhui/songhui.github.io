VFA February 2013 Update

Wed 27 February 2013
By tBunnyMan

In News.

I dont know you have all been waiting with baited breath since my last announcement and we have big news for you this time. We had our opening kickoff meeting and will continue to have meetings twice a month until we get closer to our convention. We don't want to leave you all in the dark so without further ado I will get right to the point;

2013-02 Meeting Announcements

  • We are looking at a 200 person venue for our date in Fall.
  • We will not be announcing any details until we have contracts signed.
  • We will start doing open staff interviews March 21st.
  • We have decided to start holding grill outs at the Monthly Board meet to take signups and also act as a public suggestion/forum.
  • We will start posting new content every week to this site.
    • We will be posting meeting recaps for everyone.
    • Also we will recap upcoming meets and announcements every week.

The Monthly Grill Outs

VFA is so much more than just a convention; we want to support the community as a whole. One of the ways we came up with to do this and help get people excited for the con is adding a VFA sponsored grill out to the monthly park meet we already have.

We will be brining out own grill, dogs & burgers, and a heat lamp since it's still a bit brisk. While everyone is nomming the delicious we will also be passing around signup and suggestion sheets. I will probably try to sneak in some boca burgers so my vegetarian brethren can still join in.

Weekly Announcements

We want to try and be a central and neutral repository for information for out locals and visitors. Hopefully on a weekly basis we will be posting all the open events to our site, which you can follow in your RSS reader if you choose.

If you have any announcements or events you want posted here please email staff at vegas fur dot com. We will try to watch the board and Facebook to catch things but we are only human so your help is ALWAYS appreciated. If interest is high I will put up a signup form as well.
