Membership vs open events

Thu 12 July 2012
By tBunnyMan

In Grocery.

I honestly don't know how to handle membership.

I have minor ideas here and there but they are not fully formed. My inital though is that anyone that wants to go to an event should have to be a registered memeber and to have the fee be nothing. Then we can keep track of everything for liability reasons and all that. However that seems like it would be a fiasco when trying to acheve the main goal of the events. No one has to join a group to go to AC or PawFur.

So then do we only have staff? Just the people large enough to put on the event?

Maybe we have a full membership with dues, and put on small events internally with fringe benifits like catering and cool stuff. Then the staff itself focuses the group towards working on the major big events. I think this is kind of how some cons work.

I've got ideas but not answers.
