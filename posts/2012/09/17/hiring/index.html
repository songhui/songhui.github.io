FVA is Hiring!

Mon 17 September 2012
By tBunnyMan

In News.

Vegas Furry Arts needs dedicated fuzzies excited about helping run amazing events to benefit the Souther Nevada furry and arts! Currently we are just a two man team and we've decided we don't want to black box our board or even go about the normal way of picking all of our friends for board and staff. Instead we will be going though a hiring procedure!

We are officially open for all applications! Please send your full resume and cover letter explaining what position you are looking to fill, something that you feel the community can really benefit from, and what you feel you bring to Vegas Furry Arts to staff (at) vegasfur (dot) com.

Open Positions

Here are some things to keep in mind when submitting;

  • Please remember we are not just about conventions. This group's goal is to host regular open furry events all around town such as; art shows, formals, parties, craft events, first friday, and other possible cross-community participation events.
  • Currently we are only actively hiring for the director position. Please feel free to submit for staffing positions but until we find a director and form our not for profit we will not be calling back or interviewing for anything else.
  • We are not for profit, not non-profit so there will be no payout or salary. As staff of VFA you will be expected to contribute to the running and maintenance of the group and as a director you will be expected to contribute a large share of the several thousands of dollars it takes to start and run a not for profit.
  • We are not accepting outside investors/donators currently.
  • Serious inquiries only.

Director position

Vegas Furry Arts is currently looking for a third director to help form and manage VFA. As a steering board member you will need to be able to communicate well and in a timely manner, helping drive community events, and guide the staff & events of VFA in line with it's goals of cultivating art and community in the Las Vegas Furry community. We will be working with several other furry and similar groups in town to help unify and cultivate our art.

The board will comprise of three members, which are registered members of the not for profit incorporation and will all carry equal responsibility of the future of the group fiscally until the time when a treasurer is appointed.

Qualifications

  • Staff Leadership and Management experience.
  • 1+ years in a leadership role in community events.
  • Excellent oral, written, and interpersonal skills.
  • Great additude and outlook.
  • Strong people, organization, and financial management skills.
  • Ability to work in a team and handle multiple tasks in an organized and timely manner.
  • Experience with GTD based productivity a plus.
  • Working knowledge/training on board governance will be a plus.
