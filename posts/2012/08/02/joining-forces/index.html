Joining Forces

Thu 02 August 2012
By tBunnyMan

In News.

tags: convention

In one of my better or worse moments I have been talking to CynWolf of MidFur fame. He too has visited the land of sun and never-ending entertainment and realized that Las Vegas doesn't need a furry convention it deserves one... Obviously there was a great war fought on the lines of IRC and iMessage to decide how we shalt handle the great joining of powers and style of festivities. However, once our hearts were searched and the great truth was reflected on fact remains we both wanted the same thing at heart and have decided to join teams! It will be a shaky alliance and many wars will be fought across time(zones) and space but in the end this dark alliance will allow Vegas to benefit from all the awesome power of MidFur!!!

On a more serious note we had a long talk about it. We both have separate ideals on what the con should be like, especially in the first years. In the end we will hash it out to a solid middle ground which will hopefully be the best con possible for all of the Vegas Furs.

As for VFA? I think this just means all of it's plans are accelerated. I have started talking to my top candidates for board members and people are really coming together for this one. By the end of August we should have VFA legally solidified and start working on locations, staffing, and budget. By the end of the year we should be beyond ready for our target of Nevada Day 2013 for our first convention.
