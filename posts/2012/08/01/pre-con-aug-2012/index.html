Pre-Con Ideas (The August 2012 Edition)

Wed 01 August 2012
By tBunnyMan

In News.

tags: convention

So I wanted to write up the pre-plan of the convention that VFA is planning. If anything just to have all the notes in one place for reference. We still have a bit to go to get there but more importantly we have plenty of time to do it in!

Date

  • October 2013
    • Use Nevada day holiday?
    • Start with a one day event?

Attendance & Registration

  • Cap attendance at size of one ballroom
  • All cash registration to save overhead
  • Do pre-reg at the local meets!
  • Keep registration as cheap as possible
    • Base it on ballroom rate + expense / 200 heads
  • All overages go to VFA treasury for next event
    • Zero payroll
    • Zero payouts
  • Simple laminate badges; universal & transferable
    • DefCon like registration makes it really easy
    • No worries on tracking or printing on site
    • Badges could be shared but not free replaced if lost
  • Post a waiver everyone must sign
    • Standard disclaimer and disavow of liability
    • Have a separate one for under 18 to require parent consent

Layout

  • Hold everything one one ballroom
    • This makes everything cheaper & easier to manage
    • Also prevents ghosting (no badge, no entrance)
  • Divide room with space not physical dividers.
  • Use backstage area as fursuit lounge.
  • Area for artists to hang art
  • No open adult content at all, no age separation
    • Vendors may be able to work around this with strict check-ID
  • Fursuiters will need to change in the fursuit area
    • concession to hotel, security
    • No masks or suits allowed outside con area
    • We MIGHT be able to get a small connected room for changing

Events

  • Have a few small panels focused on art
    • painting
    • anatomy
    • fursuiting Q&A
    • Fursuit building
  • Have some entertainment on the stage?
    • Vegas has some musicians
    • Live draw
  • A few discussion panels
    • Fun in vegas for all ages
    • Living furry
  • Static stuff
    • Lego table
    • Video game area
    • Tabletop game area (Ocean?)
    • Maybe fursuit/ears&tails construction area

Sweet side ideas

  • Have a build panel where someone brings in a whole lotta material and stuff and then for a fee they can join the panel and build their own ears.
  • Local artist Guest of Honor! Way cooler than popufur contest. Lets highlight the local talent!
  • Try to arrange a small cheap room block. Even though it's local targeted we still want post con room parties and to have plenty of out of town friends come in. Cheap rooms sweeten the deal.
