A convention by any other name

Thu 30 August 2012
By tBunnyMan

In News.

tags: convention

Things are really kicking into high gear over here! We are setting up to get VFA registered, have a few venues on the list of possibilities, hiring is about to open, ideas are getting hashed out, deals getting formed...

But don't have a name for our convention! This is where we reach out to the people this con is for, please help us name our new furcon!

All you need to do is use the comments to post your best name ideas. Please don't fill them up with "+1, this one, seconded, ect" replies, just post your original ideas. On Nevada Day (The Last Friday of October) we will close out the comments and pick the top three suggestions. Those three will be turned into a poll where everyone can vote on the final name for our new con!

Now here is where we sweeten the deal. The fur who originally posts the final name for the convention1 will receive our equivalent of a black badge, meaning free admission for life. How sweet is that!? Yea, as long as this convention bares the name you choose you get in for free2.

However, the first person to suggest the name PafCon will pay the winners registration for life3. ^_^

  1. Of course this blog allows for mostly anonymous comments. If you choose to be anonymous or use a name that we cannot directly and definitely link you to then your eligibility for any prize or reward is forfeited. ↩

  2. Obviously there are rules. You will be required to sign over the name and any associated copyrights over to us for liability reasons. The free registration for life only applies to you and is only valid for as long as the convention retains the name. The offer cannot be transferred, traded, or sold. You may not use this offer in conjunction with any other offers, nor may you use this to register for or on behalf of anyone else. This reward has no cash value and is not a legal agreement promising admittance or a held badge in your name. You will still be required to register as a regular attendee but at time of payment cost will be reduced to $0.00 USD. In order to claim this prize you must provide a valid state ID and your name and information will be logged with VFA. ↩

  3. LEGAL NOTICE: The first person to suggest the name PafCon will NOT have to pay the winners registration for life, that's a joke. ↩
